Supporting ‘Make in India’ Drive:

In consonance with the Prime Minister’s vision, StayHappi is a nationwide chain of retail pharmacies to support ‘Make in India’ drive with quality medicines and other healthcare products at real prices.

Current universe is dominated by brands where Marketing Companies expend exorbitant amounts of resources to drive demand of particular brands, and patients get these medicines at very high prices. StayHappi Pharmacies will fulfill Government’s aspiration to implement Uniform Code of Pharmaceuticals Marketing Practices (UCPMP) without any efforts or legislative compulsion with respect to availability of high quality medicines.

Quality Healthcare for All

Seventy-five percent of Indian population survives on a monthly income of Rs 5,000, and expensive medicines is a big burden on their pockets every month. Medicines are sold primarily as branded drugs in almost every therapeutic segment, at prices which are reasonable according to global standards, but still very high as per the Indian scenario. Branded products involve unnecessary costs like promotional material, gifts, freebies, working expenditure on huge field force, training costs, etc., which result in an increased cost of medicines to the consumers. Indian pharmaceutical market is flooded with overpriced medicines that are too expensive for the common people, since they have to bear these expenses out of their pockets. A large chunk of our country’s population has a tough time fulfilling the basic necessities of life such as food, shelter and water. Thus, access to Generic Drugs with Certified High Quality at Real Price is critical to ensure affordable and quality healthcare in India.


When it comes to saving lives and making people healthier, what matters is the quality of the medicine and the access to this medicine. This is why we will sell our products with only Generic Name and not Brand Name. We wish to be the torch-bearers of this “Revolution” in the field of Pharmaceuticals and make “Health for all” a reality by enabling Indians lead healthier and happier lives.

The StayHappi brand - as the name suggests, it’s about a wish we have, to see everyone around happy and healthy. And we would do everything we can to make sure they stay that way, always.
