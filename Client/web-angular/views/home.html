About Us

StayHappi Pharmacy is a nationwide retail pharmacy chain that provides high quality generic medicines at truly affordable prices. Our products are sold at Real Prices and offer HIGH VALUE to patients, since prices are substantially lower as compared to the branded drugs.

Supporting ‘Make in India’ Drive:

In consonance with the Prime Minister’s vision, StayHappi is a nationwide chain of retail pharmacies to support ‘Make in India’ drive with quality medicines and other healthcare products at realistic prices.

The current universe is dominated by brands where Marketing Companies expend exorbitant amounts of resources to drive demand of particular brands, and patients get these medicines at very high prices. StayHappi Pharmacies will fulfill the Government’s aspiration to implement the Uniform Code of Pharmaceuticals Marketing Practices (UCPMP) without any efforts or legislative compulsion with respect to availability of high quality medicines.

Quality Healthcare for All

Medicines are sold primarily as branded drugs in almost every therapeutic segment, at prices which are reasonable according to global standards, but still very high as per the Indian scenario. 75% of Indian population survives on a monthly income of Rs 5,000 and expensive medicines are a big burden on their pockets every month. Branded products involve unnecessary costs like promotional material, gifts, freebies, working expenditure on huge field force, training costs, etc., which result in an increased cost of medicines to the consumers. The Indian pharmaceutical market is flooded with overpriced medicines that are too expensive for the common people, since they have to bear these expenses out of their pockets. A large chunk of our country’s population has a tough time fulfilling the basic necessities of life, such as food, shelter and water. Therefore, access to Generic medicines with Certified High Quality at Real Price is critical to ensure affordable and quality healthcare in India.

When it comes to saving lives and making people healthier, what matters is the quality of the medicine and access to this medicine. This is why we provide medicines with only Generic Names and not Brand Names. We wish to be the torchbearers of this ‘Revolution’ in the field of Retail Pharmacies and make ‘Health for all’ a reality by enabling Indians lead healthier and happier lives.

The StayHappi brand - as the name suggests, it’s about a wish we have, to see everyone around happy and healthy. And we would do everything we can to make sure they stay that way, always.
